Discussion

The shape that defines a rectangular shape with a pointer. Radius – number of pixels for the rounded corners pointLocation – location of the point where the top edge starts at 45, the right edge at 135, the bottom edge at 225 and the left edge at 315. pointAngle – not fgunctional yet. Make this equal to pointLocation in order to point it in the right direction. pointSize – the square in which the pointer will be defined, should be narrower or less high than the shape minus the radiuses.

Pointers are not placed on the rounded corners.

pointSize should be less wide or high than the edge that it is placed on minus 2 * radius. radius should be smaller than the length of the edge / 2.
